OUR MISSION

Our mission is to solve the problem by providing you a unique ID, Which will be helpful to fill all of your national level forms. FOR EXAMPLE:-School and colleges admission forms, any of the competitive exam forms etc.


By this you have not to carry your forms, certification's, documents with yourselves. you have to carry just a small ID which will be generated and given by us to you.


Now, You may think that how will you get the ID and make your work this easy! So, first of all you have to complete the registration by just a click LINK, by filling a small registration form, you will get the verification process started just by your ADDHAR CARD number. So, we can verify and get you some assures of keeping all your documents safe with us. You will get to know after the ADDHAR CARD verification by getting the OTP on your email and as soon as you will enter the OTP, that soon you are going to get your unique id and the DASHBOARD, you will be able to control and fill or update your details on it.
